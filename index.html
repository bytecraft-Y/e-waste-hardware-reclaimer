Quick Salvage Guidelines

Safety Notice

Old PCBs can contain toxic lead solder, flame retardants, or chemicals. Always work in a ventilated area, wash hands, and never desolder bloated cells or mercury bulbs.

Desoldering Pro Tip:

For through-hole parts (resistors, DIP chips), use a solder vacuum pump or copper solder wick. For small SMD components, a hot air rework nozzle makes salvage quick and prevents pads from tearing.
